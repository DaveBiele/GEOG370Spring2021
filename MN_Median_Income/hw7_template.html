This is a choropleth of Median Income by County in Minnesota. The Projection is 26792, which is for central Minnesota. Stretching on counties in the northern portion of the state is visible. The darker the color the higher the median income of the county. Data was obtained from the US Census and is based on the American Community Survey Results
